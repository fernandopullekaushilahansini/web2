Sigiriya - The Lion Rock

Sigiriya or Sinhagiri (Lion Rock Sinhala: සීගිරිය, Tamil: சிகிரியா, pronounced see-gi-ri-yə) is an ancient rock fortress located in the northern Matale District near the town of Dambulla in the Central Province, Sri Lanka.

Adam’s Peak

Adam’s Peak, mountain in Southwestern Sri Lanka (Ceylon), Its conical summit terminates in an oblong platform about 74 by 24 feet (22 by 7 m), on which there is a large hollow, resembling the print of a human foot, 5 feet 4 inches by 2 feet 6 inches.
